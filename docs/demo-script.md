# Demo Script — Pathway

**Total:** 3:00

## [0:00 – 0:20] Hook

"For students whose parents went to college elsewhere, US college is a black box. Fellowships, research, clubs — other students get pointed to them by name. First-gen students don't. Pathway is the missing mentor, as a branching tree."

## [0:20 – 0:45] Intake (Maya persona)

Click "Use demo · Maya" on the welcome slide → jumps straight to the Ready step.

"Maya is a first-gen freshman, CS, on Pell grant, 8 hours a week, discovery mode — she doesn't know if she wants AI/ML or cybersec yet. Let's show her both futures."

Click "Generate my tree →".

## [0:45 – 1:15] Tree expand

Land on `/pathway`. Tree shows root + one focused seed (AI/ML Research by default).

Click a child node. Side panel slides open with why-this, why-now, todos, source URL, and a drafted outreach email to a real UCLA advisor.

"This isn't a chatbot. Every opportunity cites a real UCLA program. Every email goes to a real person. The server validates every node against our curated corpus — if Claude hallucinates, we catch it and fall back to advising staff."

## [1:15 – 1:45] The money shot — ghost rail

Point to right margin: "Alternative futures — Unchosen paths."

Click "Cybersecurity" in the ghost rail. Canvas swaps. Now the tree walks cybersec.

"Maya didn't have to commit. She walks both futures in parallel before picking. That's discovery mode."

## [1:45 – 2:30] Ethics + humility

Point to "What I might be wrong about" at bottom-right (when visible).

"Every branch surfaces uncertainty. The banner at the top warns about deadlines she's about to miss. Every leaf hands off to a real human advisor. Nothing here replaces a real mentor — it gets Maya to the right door."

Switch to Raj persona (bottom tabs) — reloads with directed mode + AI/ML PhD goal.

"For students with a concrete goal, the tree is sharper — backwards-chained from the outcome."

## [2:30 – 3:00] Close

"10 hours, Claude Sonnet 4.6, Next.js. The mentor we wish we'd had. Pathway."

Link to repo + deploy URL in the closer.
